Our Mission

Reduce

Cut down on waste by reusing items. Help the environment and also support people in need, reducing poverty.

Set up a system to share useful items with those who don't have much. We're also making it easy for people to donate extra food and money to help others.

Collaborate

Work together with groups that help people. Make it simple for anyone who wants to help by donating. Team up with donors to make a bigger impact in supporting those in need.
